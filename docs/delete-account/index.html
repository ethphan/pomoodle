Delete Your Pomoodle Account

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Delete in App (Recommended)

  1. Open Pomoodle
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete account
  4. Tap Confirm delete account

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account record (email / auth identity)
  • Your Pomodoro session history and statistics

Deletion Timing

Deletion is processed immediately after confirmation.
