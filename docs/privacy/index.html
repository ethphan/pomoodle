Pomoodle Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Pomoodle helps you run Pomodoro focus sessions and view your own session statistics.

Data We Collect

  • Account info: email address (or Google account identity via Supabase Auth)
  • App data: Pomodoro session titles, status, durations, and completion timestamps

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate your account
  • Store and sync Pomodoro sessions
  • Show your personal statistics (day/week/month/year)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We use Supabase to provide authentication and database storage.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account in-app from Settings > Delete account. This deletes your account and associated Pomodoro history.
